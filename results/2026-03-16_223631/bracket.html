Glossary

AdjEM
Adjusted Efficiency Margin. Points scored minus points allowed per 100 possessions, adjusted for opponent strength. The single best predictor of tournament success.
AdjO / AdjD
Adjusted Offensive / Defensive Efficiency. Points scored (or allowed) per 100 possessions, adjusted for opponent strength. Lower AdjD is better.
KenPom Rank
Overall team ranking from kenpom.com, based on AdjEM. #1 is the best team in the country.
Barthag
From Barttorvik. Estimated probability of beating an average Division I team on a neutral court. 0.95+ is elite.
WAB
Wins Above Bubble. How many wins above (or below) what a bubble team would achieve against the same schedule. Positive = comfortably in the tournament.
Top-25 Record
Wins and losses against top-25 ranked opponents. Reveals how a team performs against elite competition.
Leverage
Model probability / public ownership. Values >1 mean the public is undervaluing this pick. Higher leverage = more contrarian value if the pick hits.
Public %
Percentage of Yahoo Bracket Mayhem entrants picking this team to advance to this round. Represents what "the field" is doing.
P(1st)
Probability this bracket finishes 1st in the pool, estimated via Monte Carlo simulation of thousands of random tournaments against opponent brackets sampled from public ownership.
E[Score]
Expected ESPN bracket score using standard scoring: 10, 20, 40, 80, 160, 320 points per round. The championship pick alone is worth 320 points.
EMV
Expected Marginal Value. P(upset) × ownership_gain − P(chalk) × ownership_cost. Positive EMV means picking the upset increases your expected pool finish.
Confidence Tiers
🔒 Lock: ≥75% win probability. 👍 Lean: 55-75%. 🎲 Gamble: <55%.
